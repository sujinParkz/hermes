Trim 31 silky bag

4,020

THE STORY BEHIND Created in 1958 and redesigned in 2020, the Trim transforms the original shape of a horse trough into an everyday bag. Its sporty chic look with its signature snap hook clasp has made it a Hermès classic. Worn short or long, over the shoulder or as a shoulder strap, it always fits close to the body and features one or two gussets inside separated by a zipped pocket.

FIND OUT MORE ABOUT SWIFT CALFSKIN
  • This extremely supple, sophisticated leather is named after Jonathan Swift, the author of Gulliver's Travels, to highlight its resemblance to Gulliver calfskin, which is no longer featured in today's collections.
  • First appeared in the collections: 2004
  • Appearance: Almost smooth with a delicate shine; lightly marked grain that is sometimes hardly noticeable
  • Feel: Soft and tender
  • Hand: Supple and generous
  • Change over time: Becomes even more supple
