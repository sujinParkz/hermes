Simone Hermes bag

8,040

THE STORY BEHIND Names after one of Emile Hermès’ 4 daughters, the Simone Hermès bag is inspired by the shape of old school notebooks. Like a stack of books, the bag juxtaposes three compartments and joins them together with a smooth leather “belt”. Its adjustable strap allows it to be carried in the hand or on the shoulder.
