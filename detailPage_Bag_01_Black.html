THE STORY BEHIND Inspired by a 1970s design taken from the Hermès archives, the "H en Biais" motif is revealed on this light and feminine summer cabas bag. The bag is available in two graphic versions: for the day, a jacquard canvas in contrasting pastel colors, and a more unique version featuring a tufted canvas H with raised embroidery which creates a velvety look.
