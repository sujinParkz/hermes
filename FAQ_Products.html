Products

  1. AVAILABILITY

    1. Where can I purchase Hermès leather goods?

      Hermès leather goods may be purchased exclusively in Hermès stores and on the Hermès website, with the exception of the Kelly and Birkin lines, which are sold exclusively in our bricks-and-mortar stores (addresses available on fr.stores.hermes.com). Due to the high number of requests for these objects, it is possible that the model you are seeking is not available. We invite you to contact our sales consultants, who will be able to help you, or Customer Service.

    2. Why is there sometimes a delay before a leather goods item is available?

      All of our products are created by hand by our artisans, according to very strict quality criteria, and mastering Hermès expertise requires many years. We work primarily with leather, a natural material and a resource limited by Hermès’ strict selection criteria. For all of these reasons, several months are required to create an Hermès leather good.

    3. Why can't I find an item on the website?

      We kindly invite you to visit one of our physical boutiques of your choice. One of our agents will have the pleasure to let you discover our frangrances and offer you a sample if available.

    4. How may I receive a sample of your fragrance?

      We will continue providing exchanges and refunds, once the packages have been received and checked by our quality department.
      The reimbursement periods from the article 11.1 of our general conditions of sale are provided for information only. Current circumstances may disrupt our logistic activities and delivery operations, and refund operations may encounter possible delays.

    5. Can I choose the serial number of the Hermès Editeur scarf?

      If you wish to check the availability of a serial number and order an Hermès Editeur scarf, please contact our Customer Service by telephone.

  2. SIZE GUIDE

    1. Which belt size shall I order?

      Belts are sized in centimeters. In order to determine your Hermès belt size, we recommend measuring one of your own belts from the base of the tongue to the hole you use most. You can also measure your waist circumference at the point where you normally position your belts. For example, if your waist measures 85 cm, your Hermès belt size would be 85.

    4. How do I care for my Hermès bag or leather good?

      Leather is a living material that evolves, changes colour, softens, and becomes patinated over time. It should be protected from humidity and prolonged exposure to light or extreme heat. It is not recommended to use care products available on the market that are not adapted to Hermès leathers. The best way to maintain your bag is to regularly return it to our workshops to be cleaned and, if needed, restitched and repolished.

    8. How do I take care of my jewellery?

      Although it is highly resistant, precious metals and naturel stones are sensitive to their environment.
      In general, we recommend that you remove your jewellery before any activity that may damage it : sport, swimming pool or beach, but also housekeeping, gardening and DIY.
      Beware of shocks, which cause marks and scratches of varying depth. Similarly, several pieces of jewellery worn or stored in contact with each other may scratch or catch one another.
      Finally, to slow down the natural oxidation effect, avoid exposing your jewellery to moisture, intense heat, and contrasts in temperature. Make it a habit to remove your jewellery before washing your hands, and for example before a sauna or steam room session.

      Please peruse the following care tips, they will help you preserve your Hermès leather lastingly.

    1. How do I authenticate an Hermès product?

      The only way to guarantee the authenticity of an Hermès product is by making your purchase through our official website Hermes.com, at an Hermès store or through an authorized distributor (shop-in-shop within a department store, authorized watch dealer, etc.). An original invoice is created at the time of purchase for both online and in-store purchases. We cannot guarantee nor verify the authenticity of an item purchased outside of an official Hermès point of sale.

    1. Will my order be delivered in an orange box?

      All orders are delivered in an orange box tied with a bolduc ribbon, excluding fragrance, makeup and beauty products, calendar products, books, Hermès Editeur objects, large objects and certain equestrian items.   7. BESPOKE

    1. How can I order a bespoke object?

      To create a customized object, begin by providing us with a detailed explanation of what you would like. We will respond within a month with an agreement in principle, an estimate and waiting period. Our team of designers will design this unique object for you.
      To order a customized object, please send us an email with your contact information via the “Contact us” page.
      You may also visit one of our stores, where one of our sales consultants will help you and answer any questions you may have.

    2. How long does it take to create a bespoke object?

      We will be pleased to provide a price estimate for your customized object within one month, in addition to the waiting period to receive the item, once you have explained in detail your dream object or idea. Please send us an email containing your contact information.
      You may also visit one of our stores, where one of our sales consultants will help you to explain what you would like.

    3. How do I order a custom-fit saddle?

      With its saddles, Hermès seeks to bring together horse and rider.
      For a custom-fit saddle, we suggest making an appointment with one of our saddle experts to organize testing and have your equipment made to measure.
      From the first meeting to the final adjustments, we help you to choose the best model for your riding style, and adapt your saddle to suit your preferences, your body shape and your horse.
      Once you have made your choice, the saddle will be crafted by a single artisan in our workshop based at 24 Rue de Faubourg Saint Honoré. It will be delivered to you within 3 to 4 months.
      This service is complementary. For more information, consult our dedicated webpage or contact our Customer Service.

  8. APPLE WATCH HERMÈS

    1. What does GPS + Cellular mean?

      Apple Watch Hermès (GPS + Cellular) features built-in cellular, giving you the freedom to stay fully connected with just your Apple Watch. You can make and receive phone calls, reply to messages, and get notifications even when you don’t have your iPhone with you.

    2. How do I set up cellular service on my Apple Watch Hermès?

      When you pair your Apple Watch Hermès (GPS + Cellular) with your iPhone, you will be prompted to set up cellular service with your provider. Or you can set up service later using the Watch app on your iPhone.

    3. How do I receive calls on my Apple Watch Hermès (GPS + Cellular)?

      Apple Watch Hermès shares the same number as your iPhone. When you receive a phone call and your Apple Watch is set up and powered on, it rings whether or not your iPhone is nearby.

    4. How much does cellular service cost?

      While service plans vary by carrier, many offer free or special trials for Apple Watch Hermès. Learn more about carriers and promotions on www.apple.com/watch/cellular.

    5. Can I use my Apple Watch Hermès (GPS + Cellular) internationally?

      Apple Watch Hermès (GPS + Cellular) does not support international roaming.

      But you'll still be able to complete a call to emergency services almost anywhere in the world. Just press and hold the side button on Apple Watch Hermès with cellular and you'll be connected to the help you need, even if you're nowhere near your iPhone.

    6. How can I find out if my iPhone service plan is eligible for Apple Watch Hermès (GPS + Cellular)?

      You can contact your service provider for more details or see participating carriers and eligibility on Apple website.
      We recommend buying your Apple Watch Hermes in your country of residence in order to fully enjoy the features of your Apple Watch Hermes.
