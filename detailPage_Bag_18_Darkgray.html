Dynamo duffle bag

4,080

THE STORY BEHIND This extremely light and spacious sports or weekend bag, with a graphic motif inspired by speed sports, allows you to carry everything you need. The innovative technical mesh of this bag, made of jacquard weave, is used for the first time on leather goods: both flexible and resistant, it is produced in high volumes to draw the outline of the bag and its reliefs.
