THE STORY BEHIND At the beginning of the 20th century, Hermès offered extraordinary dog collar collections to its refined clientele who were offered a version with trinkets, maybe one with its precious leathers or another with its streamlined studs. Designed to protect hunting dogs elegantly, these collars inspired the house to create a belt adorned with domed, four-sided studs and a ring.
