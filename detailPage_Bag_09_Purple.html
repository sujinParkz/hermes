Hermes Cinhetic bag

THE STORY BEHIND This line of bags and clutches borrows its name from kinetic art. Inspired by the vanity cases of yesteryear, their distinct silhouette and square-ish shape evolve around a clever clasp: when locked, its mechanism draws a H, which turns, when opened, into a discreet silver line. A goldsmith’s feat made for leather works in motion.
